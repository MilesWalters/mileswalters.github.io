Fractal Addresses

Find the unique mathematical coordinate of your text on the Mandelbrot set.

Why this works:
The main body of the Mandelbrot set is a perfect Cardioid (which, according to the dictionary, is a heart-shaped curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle as it rolls around another identical circle). Every mathematical fraction between 0 and 1 represents a unique angle (0° to 360°) around this shape. By turning your text into binary, we create a fraction, which creates an angle, giving your text an exact (X, Y) physical address on the fractal!
